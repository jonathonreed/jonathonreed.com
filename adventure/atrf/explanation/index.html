A Chairs and Tables project

Dear reader,

Apart from a few minor changes, this website is a duplicate of the official project website hosted on a Chairs and Tables page. While I (Jonathon) was still cycling in northwestern British Columbia in September, I lost access to editing the website, and I didn’t regain that access upon my completion of the project. I’ve copied the site onto my own domain because it was designed, written and coded entirely by me, and I care about maintaining a version of it that I can still edit.

The vision of the project was a shared map with embedded content from both Asad and myself, but unfortunately that’s not the way it worked out. On this version of the site, I’ve archived all of my interviews, research and writing from the duration of the expedition; they will be accessible for as long as I’m around.

Jonathon Reed
19 October 2017
