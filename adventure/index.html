About Me

Adventure-based photographer and writer just beginning to take exploring seriously. My first backpacking trip was a 60-km trail when I was 11 years old and since then I’ve just kept returning to the wild. I love mountains. I seek out sunrises. I’m inspired by adventurers like Ben Saunders and Alistair Humpreys, and at the end of the day I don’t want a photo without a story to tell.
