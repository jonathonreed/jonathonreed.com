Something worth listening to

Music is one of those things that's comfortably and irrevocably part of my life. Like longboarding or eyeliner—it doesn’t define me, but it's always kind of there. It beats in my chest and vibrates under my fingertips.

I play music, sure. I also listen, sometimes really carefully. I was always the first of my siblings to ask my mom to put on a tape when we got in the van when I was little. I spent my savings as a kid on a guitar and an MP3 player. I always stop to listen to street musicians.

So this is a big part of who I am. Over the past couple years I've been working on creating ways of sharing it with other people. I've currently got a mini-project for the seasons and an indie folk music discovery archive called Vinyl & Wind.

The hope is that it will give you something worth listening to.
