Make-Believe Warriors

Indie Folk / 2013

A playlist for one of my favourite stories in children’s literature, J.M. Barrie’s Peter Pan. I made this playlist before I’d read the book, so it’s missing some of Barrie’s original nuances and is more significantly shaped by the 2003 film adaptation.

The songs are about the fleeting innocence of youth, the bittersweet loss of growing up. A few are anchored in the sunlit shores of a faraway island, others are like rain on a London street.

Part of the BOYS WORTH SINGING FOR collection.
