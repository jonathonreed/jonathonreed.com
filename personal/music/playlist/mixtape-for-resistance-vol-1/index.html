Built with love by Jonathon

Mixtape For Resistance, Vol. 1

Hip Hop / 2017

In weeks following the inauguration of Trump and the subsequent development of his racist, misogynistic, homophobic, climate-denying (etc.) administration, I had a hard time managing all the saddening, discouraging, maddening (etc.) news overwhelming my social media feeds. Words aren’t really enough. It was tough. And I knew it would continue to be.

In an effort to keep myself afloat, I pulled together some jams from some of my favourite conscious rap artists, plus some driving electronic beats. Some way to blast through the bullshit.
