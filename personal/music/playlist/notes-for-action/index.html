Notes for Action

Indie Rock / 2013

Flobots, Rage Against the Machine, Wye Oak: music for the streets, for tearing down injustice and standing up for a better world. This playlist is meant to march beside you and beat in your chest. For the marginalized and downtrodden, we are not helpless. We have a song to sing. Photo by Joanna del Popolo.
