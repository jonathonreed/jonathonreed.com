Built with love by Jonathon

Shotgun Smile

Indie Rock / 2014

Gun metal meets the devil in this collection of songs inspired by desert dust and forlorn sun. It has no sense of redemption because it’s the stories of men who never felt any shame with blood against their fingers and bullets between their teeth. Blues rock and angry folk punching like a fist in a long-forgotten saloon.
