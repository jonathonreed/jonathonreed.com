Spring

For those days when the air feels good to breathe and the morning sun rises earlier than it used to, this music is lit with apricity. With the hopeful songs of The Paper Kites, Imagine Dragons, Release the Sunbird and others, this mix will help melt the lingering snow.

Summer

This music hovers around the smell of juniper and sun-gold grasses, a set of songs above the late-afternoon buzz of cicadas in the field. Musicians like James Vincent McMorrow, Angus & Julia Stone and Noah Gunderson are arranged carefully to pass on the quiet melody of leaves in the wind.

Autumn

A playlist for cold mornings and falling leaves, for the days when you can feel a chill in the air and the sun doesn't trail her fingers across your skin quite like she used to. Take a moment, find that cup of tea and old familiar sweater, and let the songs of Roo Panes, Old Man Canyon and Alexi Murdoch hold you until the warm days come again.
